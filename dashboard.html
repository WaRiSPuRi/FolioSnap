Custom Domain
Point your own domain to your Profilr portfolio.
ℹ️ How custom domains work Fully automated custom domains require a paid Vercel plan. Follow the steps below to set one up manually — it takes about 5 minutes and is completely free if you already own a domain.
Your current portfolio URL
Publish your portfolio first
Steps to add a custom domain
1
Buy a domainGet a domain from Namecheap, Google Domains, or any registrar. e.g. yourname.dev
2
Add it to VercelGo to your Vercel project → Settings → Domains → Add your domain (e.g. yourname.dev)
3
Add DNS recordIn your domain registrar, add a CNAME record: www → cname.vercel-dns.com and an A record: @ → 76.76.21.21
4
Wait ~10 minutesDNS propagates automatically. Vercel handles the SSL certificate. Your portfolio will then be live at your custom domain.
